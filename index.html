About Sikap

Sikap is a student-focused productivity and career guidance platform designed to help young learners discover their strengths, track personal goals, and explore opportunities that align with their future aspirations. Our goal is to empower students to build confidence, take action, and shape their paths with intention.

We Offer

Goal Tracker

Helps users plan and monitor their progress.

Career Path Finder

Recommends careers based on interests and strengths.

Opportunity Hub

Connects users to job searches, trainings, and opportunities.
